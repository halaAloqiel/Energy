Energy Mix

The energy mix of a country is the combination of different primary energy sources that make up the total primary energy supply. Our world gets our primary energy sources from burning fossil fuels. The burning of fossil fuels comes at a great cost, as it is the main contributer to global warming. Further, as the demand for energy increases, the need to shift from fossil fuels to clean energy also inceases.

To reduce global emissions we need to drift away from energy systems that are depandant on fossil fuels to low-carbon sources of energy. But, as the demand for energy is growing rapidly, the speed and scale of the energy transition that we need is therefore a new challenge.

Saudi Energy mix

His Majesty the King has launched The National Renewable Energy Program. The National Renewable Energy Program aims to increase the use of renewables in the energy mix. Further, it presents new oppurtunities for growth, as it would introduce a new industry for renewable energy technology.

Furthermore, The King Abdullah City for Atomic and Renewable Energy are strategizing a plan that would intruduce the use of atomic energy in the mix. A diverse energy mix would benefit the economy and environment, maximize the upkeep of petroleum resources for future generation, and provide more power for desalination.

Optimum Energy Mix

The energy mix to produce electricity aims to increase generation efficiency and lower costs through the diversification of energy sources used to produce electricity, depending on the available technologies and strategic direction of the sector including emission reduction. The energy mix is basically anchored on removing liquid fuel from the system in order to diversify the energy mix comprised of renewables and gas, each with a share of 50% by 2030.
